# LimonPOS Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Introduction

LimonPOS is a Point of Sale (POS) application for restaurants and retail businesses. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

## Data We Collect

### 1. Account & Authentication

- **User PIN** – Used locally to identify staff at the device. PINs are stored securely on the device and (when configured) may be synced with your backend server for authentication.

### 2. Transaction Data

- **Orders** – Table numbers, product names, quantities, prices, modifiers, notes
- **Payments** – Amounts, payment methods, timestamps
- **Tables** – Occupancy status, reservation information

### 3. Device & Sync Data

- **Device ID** – A unique identifier used to sync this device with your backend server
- **Heartbeat** – Periodic signals to indicate the device is online

## How Data Is Stored

- **On-device** – Orders, products, tables, users, and settings are stored locally in an encrypted database on the device.
- **On your server** – When configured, data is synced to your own backend server. LimonPOS does not send data to third-party analytics or advertising servers unless you integrate such services yourself.

## Cookies and Tracking

LimonPOS is a native Android app and does **not** use cookies or web tracking.  
If you access a web backoffice or dashboard linked to LimonPOS, that website may use cookies according to its own privacy policy.

## Data Sharing

LimonPOS does not share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising.  
Data may be sent only to:

- **Your backend server** – When you configure a server URL in the app
- **Google Play Services** – For app updates and basic crash reporting (if enabled in your Play Console settings)

## Data Retention

- **Local data** – Stored on the device until you clear it or uninstall the app.
- **Server data** – Retained according to your backend and business policies.

## Security

- PINs and sensitive data are stored securely on the device.
- Data transmitted to your server uses HTTPS when available.
- We recommend keeping the app and device updated and protecting access with screen lock.

## Your Rights

- You can uninstall the app at any time to remove local data.
- For data on your backend, contact your system administrator.
- If you have questions about how your business uses LimonPOS data, contact your organization’s data controller.

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact your system administrator or the person responsible for your LimonPOS deployment.
